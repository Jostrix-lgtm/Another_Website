Use A Dictionary To Learn Radicals

For example, Oriole (黄鹂) is a kind of bird （鸟）, think it is related to "bird", through "bird （鸟）" radical to find the correct Chinese character, understand its meaning.


  1. Some words can be divided into two parts,which part is the radical to look up the corresponding radical.

    2. For example,the fox (狐) can be divided into left and right parts.It is obvious that "犭" is a radical.

    What if both parts are radical? Extract the radical from the left,top and outside position of the word.

    For example,the"army" (军) emphasize the element above rather than the one below; it is "冖"

    For example, "Liu" (刘) takes left rather than right, and the radical is "wen" （文）.

    If "prisoner" (囚) take outside do not take inside, radical is "kou" （囗）.


  2. The word itself is a radical,and the radical is itself.

    3. A character whose radical is itself belongs to a single character.Such as sun (日), moon （月）, mountain （山）, water （水）, cattle （牛）, sheep （羊）, dog （犬）, human （人）,etc.are all pictographs. Pictographic characters evolved from pictographic characters and are the most primitive method of character formation.The methods of Chinese character formation include pictograph, reference, meaning, shape and sound.


  3. Searches for the first stroke of a Chinese character

    4. Encounter can not find the corresponding radical of the word,you can look up the first stroke of the word,such as"kui （亏）", "Shen （申）", "I （我）"

    However,there are some special cases,such as the root of "xiang （乡）" is "乛",but parents find "乙" on the Internet.

    This is because "乛" is a traditional Chinese character,while "乙" is a simplified version of "乛".
