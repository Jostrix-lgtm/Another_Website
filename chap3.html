Expressions of personal pronouns


Pronouns are words that replace nouns,verbs,adjectives,quantifiers and adverbs.They can be divided into three categories:personal pronouns, interrogative pronouns and demonstrative pronouns.


Modern Chinese pronouns

  • 人称代词 (Personal pronoun)： 你 (you) , 我 (me) , 他 (him) , 它 (it) , 咱们 (us) , 大家 (ourselves) , 自己 (myself)
  • 指示代词 (Demonstrative pronouns)： 这 (here) , 那 (there) , 这儿 (here) , 那边 (there) , 各 (each) , 每 (every)
  • 疑问代词 (Interrogative pronouns)： 谁 (who) , 什么 (what) , 哪 (where) , 怎么 (how) , 哪里 (where) , 几 (a few)


    • Pronouns in ancient Chinese

    A pronoun is a word that takes the place of or indicates a person or thing. Pronouns in ancient Chinese can be divided into five categories: personal pronouns, demonstrative pronouns, interrogative pronouns, indeterminate pronouns and special pronouns. Their main function is to act as subject,object, attribute or complement in a sentence.

    • 人称代词 (Personal pronoun)：朕(me) 我(me) 吾(me) 予 余 尔 女 汝(you) 若 而 乃 之 其 厥
    • 指示代词 (Demonstrative pronouns)：是 之 此 斯 兹 彼 夫 其
    • 疑问代词 (Interrogative pronouns)：谁 (who) 孰 何 曷 奚 胡 恶 安 焉
    • 特殊代词 (Special pronouns):者 所

    Daily-life expressions

    1. 你好！(Hello!)
    2. 嗨，马克。你好吗？(Hi，Mark. How are you doing？)
    3. 早上 [下午 , 晚上] 好，约翰。(Good morning [afternoon，evening], John.)
    4. 我要去上学了。(I am going to school.)
    5. 我正在吃午饭。(I'm having lunch.)
    6. 我想看电视。(I want to watch TV.)
    7. 我要睡觉了。( I'm going to bed.)
    8. 我想和你交朋友。(I want to make friends with you.)
