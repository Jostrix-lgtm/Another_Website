Learning with Panda Literacy



When you create an account for your child, you will automatically receive 3 free trial class to the account. Every Panda Literacy trial class might be different, as we will tailor the content to the interests and language level of your child.

For a 2-3 years old child, she/he will have a chance to explore new languages and taste the content and teaching. For a 4-6 years old child, they will learn more about daily languages, vocabulary words etc, which will be helpful for setting up a good starting point for future learning.



Panda Literacy offers one of the most comprehensive Chinese language learning curriculums in the industry, and no matter your child has no prior Chinese language experiences, or has spoke Chinese casually as a second language, we can always find the right curriculum for you. Just start by booking a Free Trial, and our professional Course Consultant will work closely with you and your child to recommend the best curriculum for you.



We recommend taking at least two classes per week. Learning a language is an incremental, cumulative process, and it takes time and reinforcement to learn. Spending time learning and practicing the language frequently each week will decrease the likelihood of forgetting what you have studied.

Pricing and Purchase



We accept payment through major credit cards including VISA, Mastercards, American Express, and others. For certain regions, we will also accept Buy-Now-Pay-Later options. Please talk to your Course Consultant for details.



After purchasing our class packages, we will assign a Learning Advisor to accompany your continuous learning journey. You can schedule your regular learning sessions with your Learning Advisor, and normally you can start learning right after your Learning Advisor is assigned.



Based on the product you purchase, we will have associated refund policies.

Please consult your Course Consultant for specific policies.
